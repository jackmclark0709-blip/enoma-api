Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Enoma ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit enoma.io or use our services to create and host a business website.

Information we collect

When you create an account or use Enoma, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your business name, location, phone number, and service information
  • Content you provide to build your website (about text, services, photos)
  • Basic analytics data about how your website is viewed (page views, contact clicks)
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe, not stored by Enoma

How we use your information

  • To create and host your Enoma business website
  • To send you account and billing communications
  • To send you a monthly performance report for your website
  • To improve Enoma's product and service quality

Your business website is public

The information you provide to build your website — your business name, services, location, phone number, and photos — will be publicly visible on your Enoma website. This is the purpose of the service. Do not include any information you don't want to be publicly accessible.

Analytics tracking

We track page views and contact clicks on your business website so we can show you how many people are finding and interacting with your site. We do not collect or store visitors' personal information. IP addresses are hashed before storage and never used to identify individuals.

We also use Google Analytics on enoma.io for general site usage tracking. You can opt out of Google Analytics at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Third-party services

We use the following trusted services to operate Enoma:

  • Supabase — database and user authentication
  • Vercel — website hosting and delivery
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • OpenAI — AI-powered website content generation
  • Google — Maps, Places data, and analytics

Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and data by emailing [email protected]. Note that deleting your account will take your business website offline.

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication (to keep you logged in) and analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect functionality.

Children's privacy

Enoma is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Enoma after changes constitutes acceptance.
