Common questions

Do I need a credit card to get started?

No. Your first month is completely free with no payment information required. You only need a credit card if you decide to subscribe after your trial.

When does my free trial end?

Your free month starts the day your website goes live. You'll see the exact expiry date on your website after it's created, and we'll remind you before it ends.

What happens if I don't subscribe?

After your free month, your website will show as inactive to visitors. It won't be deleted — subscribe at any time to bring it back live.

Is there a contract?

No. Enoma is month-to-month. Cancel from your dashboard at any time — no fees, no questions asked.

How does payment work?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. Enoma never stores your card details. You're billed monthly on the date you subscribe.

Is this a full website or just a listing?

It's your own standalone business website — not a listing inside a directory like Yelp or Angie's List. No competitors next to you, no ads. Just your business, on your own page.

Can I update my website?

Yes. Log into your dashboard at any time to update your services, contact info, photos, and more. Changes go live immediately.
